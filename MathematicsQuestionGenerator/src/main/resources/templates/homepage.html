Welcome to MathVerse

Welcome to MathVerse, a mathematics question generator website.

The aim of MathVerse is to support students who require further practice in the Mathematics for Computing Professionals (CS1MCP) Module.

MathVerse will test the taught concepts of the module, in the form of quizzes for individual topics, as well as practice questions for individual topics.
